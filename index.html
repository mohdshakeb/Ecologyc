GOOD DESIGN IS INHERENTLY SUSTAINABLE

We believe that good design is inherently sustainable.We work with architects and developers to develop a holistically sustainable solution for projects sited in either an urban or rural scenario, irrespective of whether they want to pursue a green building rating or not.

OUR SERVICES

Architecture

Our architectural services include 3 phases – conceptual design, design development and construction administration. We provide you with services such as program development, feasibility studies, space planning, aesthetics and look and feel of the project, construction drawings and documents, site execution assistance and project management.

Green Building Certification

We evaluate and help design the building with respect to the Green Building rating categories such as Site Planning, Water Conservation, Energy Efficiency, Materials and Resources, Indoor Environmental Quality and Innovation. We offer USGBC – LEED, IGBC and GRIHA ratings to our customers. We provide comprehensive cost-benefit analysis of green design strategies for the project.

LANDSCAPE
DESIGN

We provide landscaping services which include hardscape, softscape, outdoor furniture, pavilions, fountains, manmade ponds, sculptures and outdoor lighting. We specialize in eco-landscaping which includes organic farming, setting up of composting pits and sizing biogas plants, rainwater harvesting, aquaponics, fish farming, reed bed system, use of native species and setting up of fish tanks in interior spaces (residential or commercial).

Solar Passive
ArchitecturAL Design

Solar Passive architectural Design includes evaluating the orientation and form of the building, amount of glazing, design of shading devices for different facades (North, South, East and West), and amount of daylight in the building.

ENERGY
MODELLING

External heat gains are from the roofs, wall, windows and ground floor. Internal gains are due to people, equipment and lighting. Whole building simulation can be conducted to determine the impact of both passive design strategies and active design strategies on the annual electricity consumption of the building. Electricity savings and payback period aid the designer to select design strategies.

THERMAL
MODELLING

Thermal modelling for window assemblies is used to ascertain the thermal performance of not only the glass but the entire assembly which may include the frame, sash, jamb, muntin, spacers and sealant. Thermal Modelling is particularly helpful for retrofitting in existing buildings.

RUJU RATHOD

Ruju Rathod is a practicing architect and sustainable design analyst, specializing in green buildings and energy modelling. Ruju has done her Master of Science (M.S.) Sustainable Design from Carnegie Mellon University, Pittsburgh and Bachelor of Architecture (B Arch.) from Kamla Raheja Vidyanidhi Institute of Architecture and Environmental Studies (K.R.V.I.A), Mumbai. She is a member of the United Green Building Council and a USGBC LEED accredited professional along with being a GRIHA Trainer and Master Trainer for the Energy Conservation Building Code.

Ruju has over 9 years of experience as a practising professional in the realm of architecture and sustainable design.

She has worked at reputed companies like Inglese Architecture & Engineering and Kirksey in USA and Edifice Consultants Pvt. Ltd in India. She has worked on over 25 USGBC – LEED, IGBC and GRIHA Projects. She has been a team member for the 1st runner up entry for master planning for a 742 acre campus for I.I.T at Jodhpur. One of her projects won the IREM (Institute of Real Estate Management) LEED Stars award, USA. She has several speaking engagements to her credit and articles published in the Indian Architect and Builder magazine, Buildotech and Waste Recycling India.

She is currently a visiting faculty at Kamla Raheja Vidyanidhi Institute of Architecture and Environmental Studies.
